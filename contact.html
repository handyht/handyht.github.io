About Me.

Hi, my name is Handy! I'm a photographer and videographer born and raised in Southern California, now based in the greater Seattle area.

My love for telling stories stemmed from early childhood — my parents had a habit of always taking pictures and videos, whether it was first steps, the first day of school, or just everyday life. Watching those family home videos sparked something that never faded.

What started as nostalgia became a passion project among many other things. When I'm not behind the camera, I'm probably hunting down the best food spots, geeking out over the latest tech, or planning my next trip. I'm always looking to learn more and share what I create along the way.
